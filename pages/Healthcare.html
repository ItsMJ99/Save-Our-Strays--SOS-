Health Care

Veterinary Care

All animals in our care receive the highest standard of veterinary care. Upon arrival at our facility, each animal undergoes a thorough health examination by a licensed veterinarian. This initial assessment helps us identify any immediate medical needs and create tailored treatment plans. Regular veterinary check-ups are an integral part of our commitment to their well-being. These check-ups include vaccinations, dental care, and routine wellness exams. We believe that preventative care is key to maintaining the health of our rescued animals. Any medical issues or injuries that arise are promptly addressed with the appropriate treatments and medications, ensuring that our animals receive the best care possible.

Medical Fund

Donations play a crucial role in maintaining our dedicated medical fund, which covers a wide range of essential expenses. These contributions directly impact the lives of the animals in our care, allowing us to provide medical attention when it's needed most. The medical fund supports various aspects of healthcare, including surgeries, medications, emergency treatments, and ongoing medical care. For example, surgeries may be necessary to address injuries or conditions that require surgical intervention. Medications are provided to manage chronic health issues and to ensure the comfort of our animals. Emergency treatments are available for unexpected health crises.

Wellness Tips

We understand that responsible pet ownership extends beyond rescue, which is why we offer wellness tips to help pet owners provide the best possible care for their animals. Our advice covers a range of topics, from nutrition and exercise to grooming and mental stimulation. Proper nutrition is essential for the health and longevity of your pet. We provide guidance on choosing the right diet and establishing feeding schedules. Exercise is equally important, and we offer tips on how to ensure your pet gets the physical activity they need to stay fit and happy. Additionally, grooming and mental stimulation are crucial aspects of pet care. We provide guidance on grooming practices and activities to keep your pet mentally engaged and stimulated.

Emergency Care Information

Knowing what to do in a pet emergency can save lives. We provide clear instructions on how to recognize signs of distress or illness in animals, and we detail the steps to take during an emergency.
