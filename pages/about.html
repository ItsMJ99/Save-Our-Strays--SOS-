About Us

Welcome to Save Our Stray, where compassion meets action to make a difference in the lives of our furry friends. At Save Our Stray, we are dedicated to providing a safe haven for stray animals and working tirelessly to find them loving forever homes. Our mission is to reduce the suffering of homeless animals and create a world where every pet has a place to call home.

  • We work tirelessly to rescue stray animals from the streets, providing them with immediate medical attention and shelter.
  • Our team of dedicated veterinarians and caregivers ensures that every rescued animal receives the care they need to recover physically and emotionally.
  • Our emergency response team is prepared to step in and ensure the safety and well-being of animals affected by unforeseen circumstances
  • We actively seek loving forever homes for our rescued animals, carefully matching them with responsible and caring adoptive families.
  • We believe in giving a voice to those who cannot speak for themselves and work tirelessly to ensure that animals are treated with the respect and care they deserve.
  • In times of natural disasters or emergencies, we are there to provide assistance and shelter for animals in need.
